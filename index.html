About Me

I am a data and analytics professional with a strong foundation in business problem-solving, stakeholder engagement, and data interpretation, now transitioning into a Business Analyst role.

My career began in enterprise technology and analytics, working as a Data Analyst at Morgan Stanley and later as a Senior System Engineer at Infosys, where I supported data-driven client initiatives and developed analysis scripts to enable better business insights. These roles shaped my analytical thinking and structured approach to understanding business requirements.

Over time, I moved into education, training, and program leadership, where I led large-scale initiatives in AI, Data Analytics, Robotics, and STEM education across India. As a Program Coordinator at CoGrad, I designed and delivered technology programs in collaboration with I-Hub, IIT Delhi, managing over 100+ CSR-funded projects and coordinating with multiple stakeholders including educators, institutions, and partners.

Currently, as a Data Science Trainer at InGrade, I mentor learners in analytics, business problem-solving, and logical thinking—skills that directly align with requirement analysis, stakeholder communication, and insight generation.

I am now focused on transitioning fully into Business Analyst roles where I can leverage:

  • Strong analytical foundations
  • Experience translating business needs into structured solutions
  • Clear communication with technical and non-technical stakeholders
